Inspiration Behind my Screen Printing Design

In Mr. Sinusi's class, the main inspiration behind my screen printing design was one word "Truce", reflecting my desire to explore themes of peace, forgiveness and the resolution of conflict. THe reasson why i choose this topic is because its important to remind people that we can always find peaceful way's to solve our disagreements.

Steps I Took

  • Measuring, marking, and cutting wood (with the miter saw)
  • Gluing and stapling wood corners together to make frame
  • Cutting, stretching, stapling, hammering, and taping mesh to your frame
  • Cutting and weeding a stencil of your digital design with vinyl cutting machine
  • Heat transfer press (iron) your stencil to mesh and block "ink leak" with tape
