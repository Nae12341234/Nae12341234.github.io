Here are some steps i took to acomplish my screen printing desighn:

Steps I Took

  • Measuring, marking, and cutting wood (with the miter saw)
  • Gluing and stapling wood corners together to make frame
  • Cutting, stretching, stapling, hammering, and taping mesh to your frame
  • Cutting and weeding a stencil of your digital design with vinyl cutting machine
  • Heat transfer press (iron) your stencil to mesh and block "ink leak" with tape
